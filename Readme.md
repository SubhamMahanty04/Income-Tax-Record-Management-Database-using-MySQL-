Income Tax Record Management System using MySQL

Project Description:
The Income Tax Record Management System is a database-driven project developed using MySQL Workbench to manage taxpayer records, income details, advance tax payments, and final 
tax liabilities. The system helps in storing taxpayer information such as PAN number, contact details, and financial income records for different financial years.
This project maintains records of advance tax payments made by taxpayers during a financial year. Based on the total income and advance tax paid, the system calculates the final payable tax amount. If the advance tax paid exceeds the actual tax liability, the excess amount is automatically recorded and carried forward to the next financial year for adjustment.

Objective:
- To store taxpayer information securely
- To maintain income details for each financial year
- To record advance tax payments
- To calculate final tax payable amount
- To adjust excess advance tax for next financial year

Technologies Used:
- MySQL Workbench
- SQL (Structured Query Language)
- Relational Database Management System (RDBMS)

Modules:
- Taxpayer Management
- Income Record Management
- Advance Tax Payment Tracking
- Tax Payment Status Management
- Tax Adjustment for Next Financial Year

Conclusion:
This system efficiently manages taxpayer financial records and helps in accurate tax computation. It reduces manual effort and maintains transparency in tax payment and adjustment process.
